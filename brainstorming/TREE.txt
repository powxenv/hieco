File Naming Conventions
========================

1.  **Format:** kebab-case (lowercase with hyphens)
2.  **Semantic Names:** Descriptive of content
3.  **Numbered Folders:** Logical progression (01→02→03)
4.  **YAML Frontmatter:** Metadata on every document

Frontmatter Schema
==================

```yaml
---
title: Document Title
description: One-line summary
category: overview|bounty-research|proposals
created: YYYY-MM-DD
status: draft|complete|superseded
tags: [tag1, tag2, tag3]
related:
  - path/to/related-document.md
---
```
